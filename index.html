Who are we?

Concrete To Gardens is a non-profit, based in Bridgeport, CT, aimed at prioritzing plant and farming knowledge in the greater Bridgeport area.

Focusing on urban areas that have been designated as "food deserts," our community is doing the work of rethinking our relationship to food and its growing process.

You may know us for our community gardens, but we do so much more!

Look out for our upcoming educational workshop series, our teen farmer fellowship deadline, and our end-of-the-year potluck at Seaside Park!
